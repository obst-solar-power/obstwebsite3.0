About us

O.B.S.T Solar Power Company is a leading provider of renewable energy solutions, specializing in solar power systems, with a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship.

  • Sustainable Energy Solutions
  • Reduced Carbon Footprint
  • Environmental Conservation
  • Community Engagement

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in solar power technology, revolutionizing the energy industry and enabling a sustainable future for generations to come. By developing innovative and efficient solar solutions, we aim to empower individuals and businesses to embrace renewable energy and achieve their energy independence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to accelerate the transition to a clean and renewable future by harnessing the power of the sun. We are committed to reducing carbon emissions and combating climate change by providing sustainable and environmentally friendly energy solutions.

O.B.S.T Solar Power Company is a trusted name in the renewable energy sector, with a proven track record in delivering sustainable and environmentally friendly energy solutions. With our dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we are at the forefront of solar power technology, empowering individuals and businesses to embrace a sustainable future.

OBST Solar Power Company Plant is located in DG100 Industry Park, Gaobu Town, Dongguan City, known as the "Manufacturing Capital of China" and it’s headquarters in Nigeria is at 9, QMB Builder’s mart Road,Lekki ,Lagos State. The company was established in 2009.

Since its establishment, OBST has won many honors in the industry and obtained 20-30 intellectual property patents and software copyrights. It has passed CE certification of the European Union, ROHS certification and many industry certifications, becoming an influential brand in the energy industry.

In order to better serve customers around the world, OBST Company has set up offices in Australia, Nigeria and Malaysia respectively. While expanding the market, it provides timely and effective services to partners.

With a team of highly skilled and experienced professionals, O.B.S.T possesses extensive knowledge and expertise in solar power systems. We offer a wide range of products and services to meet the diverse energy needs of our customers. Our team of certified solar installers is dedicated to delivering high-quality solar installations tailored to the specific requirements of our customers. From system design and engineering to installation and maintenance, we provide a seamless and hassle-free experience.

O.B.S.T is vertically integrated, with its own solar panel manufacturing facility. This enables us to source components directly from our own production line, ensuring the highest standards of quality and availability. Our solar panels are engineered to provide optimal performance and withstand harsh weather conditions. Inverters are an essential component of any solar power system. At O.B.S.T, we offer state-of-the-art solar inverters that optimize energy conversion efficiency and maximize power output. Our inverters are reliable, robust, and future-proof, ensuring optimal performance for years to come.

With the rise of renewable energy, energy storage has become increasingly important. O.B.S.T offers a range of solar battery storage solutions, allowing customers to store excess energy generated by their solar panels for later use. Our battery storage systems are robust, durable, and designed to provide reliable energy storage solutions.

O.B.S.T provides advanced solar monitoring and control systems, enabling customers to monitor their solar energy production, energy consumption, and system performance in real-time. Our monitoring systems provide valuable insights and offer remote control capabilities, allowing customers to optimize their energy usage and manage their solar power systems efficiently.
